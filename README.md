## 🧠 About me

I'm a **Software Engineer and AI Adoption Lead** at **MTN Bénin**. I'm also completing a **Master's in Artificial Intelligence** at the Dakar Institute of Technology (DIT).

I build fullstack platforms and AI-powered tools that serve the African market, with a deep focus on **women's empowerment**, **community impact**, and **human-centered AI adoption**.

- 🏢 **AI Adoption Lead** at MTN Bénin
- 🎓 **Master's in AI** at the Dakar Institute of Technology (Deep Learning, Computer Vision, IoT)
- 🏆 **1st Prize** at the Hackathon IA Multimodal Bénin 2024
- 👩‍💻 **Co-founder** of ELLES (women's health app, 3,000+ users, 10 countries) & DOXÀ (postpartum wellness)
- 🌍 **Community leader** : Women Techmakers Abomey-Calavi & Cotonou, iSHEERO, IndabaX Bénin, WICSI, Alumni IFRI
- 📍 Based in **Cotonou, Bénin 🇧🇯**
